About

What makes a great a web design? A great web design prioritizes the user's needs. This involves understanding the target audience and tailoring the design to meet their preferences and expectations. Great web design is not just about the visual appearance. It's an amalgamation of aesthetics, user experience, functionality, and purpose. As web technologies and user expectations evolve, the criteria for great web design might shift, making it essential for designers to stay updated and continuously adapt.
